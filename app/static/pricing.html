How it works

  • Free recovery audit. We look at your recent failed-payment volume and estimate what is realistically recoverable, in real money, before you commit to anything.
  • We set it live. PayPilot connects to your billing, diagnoses each failed charge, schedules the right retry, and drafts the recovery message - so involuntary churn stops leaking revenue.
  • You pay on results. Billing is a share of what actually comes back. If nothing is recovered, there is nothing to pay.

Why performance-based

Failed subscription payments are measurable money. Pricing against recovered revenue means our incentive is simply to recover more of yours - there is no seat fee, no long lock-in, and no risk in trying it.
